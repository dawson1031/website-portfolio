Magic 8 Ball

This "Magic 8 Ball" project uses Javascript and jQuery to make the page emulate a real Magic 8 Ball. AS with a reall Magic 8 Ball, the viewer is propted by the Magic 8 Ball to "Ask Me Anything". When the viewer clicks the button, a window appears, and propts the viewer to type a question into the box. Once a question is asked, the Magic 8 Ball shakes and reveals the answer!
